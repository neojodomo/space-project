Fun Facts About Space

Explore the wonders and weirdness of the universe

1. A Day on Venus is Longer Than Its Year

Venus takes about 243 Earth days to rotate once on its axis, but it only takes 225 Earth days to complete one orbit around the Sun. That means a day on Venus is longer than its year!

2. Space Smells Like Burnt Steak

Astronauts returning from spacewalks have reported that their suits smelled like seared meat or welding fumes, likely due to high-energy particles interacting with materials in space.

3. Neutron Stars Can Spin at 600+ Times Per Second

Neutron stars, the collapsed cores of massive stars, can spin at incredibly high speeds — some even exceed 600 revolutions per second due to conservation of angular momentum.

4. The Moon is Slowly Drifting Away from Earth

The Moon is moving about 3.8 centimeters away from Earth every year. Eventually, this will affect the length of a day on Earth — but not for billions of years.

5. There May Be More Stars Than Grains of Sand

It’s estimated that there are more stars in the observable universe than grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth. That’s over 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 (a sextillion) stars!
